# Sample Topic: The Untold Story of Tesla's Roadster

## Topic Overview
The fascinating story behind Tesla's first car and how it almost killed the company.

## Why This Topic?
- Evergreen content
- Appeals to tech and business audiences
- Unique angle: Most people don't know the full story
- Rich with drama and unexpected twists

## Key Points to Cover
1. The partnership with Lotus
2. The engineering nightmare
3. The $140,000 per car cost
4. How it nearly bankrupted Tesla
5. The lesson that saved the company

## Sources
- Tesla blog posts (2008-2010)
- Elon Musk interviews
- Engineering reports
- News articles from the era

## Target Length: 18-22 minutes
